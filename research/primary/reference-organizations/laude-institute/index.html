Computer scientists stand at a hinge moment. Engineered intelligence is no longer theoretical. Research breakthroughs are reshaping every aspect of human society. If we choose our targets carefully, our industry has an opportunity to move all of humanity forward.

Built by and for impact researchers, Laude Institute operates at the intersection of academia and industry to catalyze research that reaches people's hands. We convene, resource, and amplify computer scientists that ship their research and impact the lives of billions.
